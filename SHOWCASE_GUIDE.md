## 🌐 3. Portfolio Case Study Outline

*When adding this to your personal portfolio website, use this structure to tell the story of the project to visitors.*

**1. Project Overview:**
"Explore Bangalore is an interactive tourism directory and PWA designed to modernize the way users discover points of interest."

**2. The Problem:**
"Tourists often struggle to juggle maps, destination info, and ride-hailing apps simultaneously. Secure, personalized, and performant directories are surprisingly rare."

**3. The Solution (Your App):**
"A centralized hub with OAuth security, an interactive Leaflet.js map, localized AI recommendation sorting, and deep-linked one-click Uber booking integration."

**4. Technical Challenges Overcome:**
*   *Challenge:* Handling geographic proximity dynamically.
*   *Solution:* Implemented the Haversine formula to compute distances from the user's browser location to over 100 coordinates instantly, driving the "Nearest to You" UI tags.
*   *Challenge:* Routing without a framework like React.
*   *Solution:* Mastered multi-file architecture with Javascript Modules and structured Vercel routing rules to emulate a Single Page App feel while maintaining discrete files.

**5. Future Roadmap:**
"Plan to integrate live external APIs for real-time weather and traffic, and expand database via user-submitted MongoDB locations."

## 🎥 4. Video Walkthrough Strategy (For LinkedIn/YouTube)

Recording a 60-90 second video of your app is the **most effective** way to get attention. Here is a suggested script/flow:

1.  **[0:00 - 0:10] Introduction:** Show the stunning login screen. Mention the Google OAuth integration. Proceed to log in smoothly.
2.  **[0:10 - 0:30] The Dashboard & Animations:** Scroll through the main list. Point out the custom CSS styling, the responsive grid, and the dynamic "Nearest to you!" distance calculations updating on the cards.
3.  **[0:30 - 0:45] Map Integration:** Open the Map View. Show how all 100+ points are clustered and plotted via Leaflet.js.
4.  **[0:45 - 0:60] AI Sorting & Filters:** Toggle the "AI Recommendations" switch to show how the logic dynamically re-orders the DOM. Select a category filter (e.g., Parks or Temples).
5.  **[0:60 - 0:80] The Uber Integration (The WOW factor):** Click on a location, open the beautiful modal (mention the glassmorphism blurred background!). Highlight the estimated cab fare, click "Ride with Uber," and show how it seamlessly attempts to deep-link to the native app.
